✂️ Required Material Checklist

  • Outer Fabric: Sized for body, sleeves, collar, and side closure loops.
  • Lining Fabric: Sleek patterned fabric for interior protection.
  • Collar Core: Heavy canvas or duck fabric for neckline structural rigidity.
  • Paper Pattern: Printed layout pattern scaled and taped together.

🥋 The Golden Rules of Lined Haoris

  • Always water-shrink your outer fabrics before cutting to prevent uneven shrinking later.
  • Machine-stitch straight through the paper pattern on side seams to lock slippery lining cuts perfectly in place.
  • Women's styles require a 3mm snippet open slot at the armpit (Miyatsu-guchi) and only a half-stitched back seam. Men's style back seams are sewn fully shut.
